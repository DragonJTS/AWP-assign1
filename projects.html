Projects!

SnakeIt

A recreation of the classic "Snake" game for the Windows Phone. Eat as much food as you can without hitting yourself or a wall. The more you eat, the longer you get!

Asteroid Field

A forced sidescroller for the Windows Phone. Dodge the asteroid while collecting the minerals. Last as long as you can!

Zombie Runner

A top down game for the Windows Phone. Avoid zombies for as long as you can! The longer you survive, the more zombies will come in search for your delicious brains!
